Step 01 — Install Extractor
1

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar

Show your browser's bookmarks bar with Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or ⌘+Shift+B (Mac), then drag the button below into it.

2

Open WhatsApp Web → Your Group → Group Info

Go to web.whatsapp.com, open the group (e.g. eCom Master's Family), click the group name at the top to open Group Info panel on the right.

3

Scroll down to the Participants section, then click your bookmarklet

In the Group Info panel, scroll until you see the Participants list with phone numbers visible. Then click the IBI Extractor bookmark — a control panel will appear.

4

Click "Start Extraction" and wait ~5 minutes for 931 members

The extractor auto-scrolls the participant list, capturing all phone numbers. When complete, click Copy All or Download CSV, then paste the numbers back here in Step 3.
